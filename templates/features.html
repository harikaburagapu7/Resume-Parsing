Features of the Resume Analyzer

  • Text Extraction: Supports PDF and DOCX formats to extract text from resumes.
  • Similarity Analysis: Uses BERT embeddings and cosine similarity to match resumes with job descriptions.
  • Evaluation Metrics: Displays precision, recall, and F1 score metrics.
